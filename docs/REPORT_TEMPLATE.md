# Report Template

Ban co the dua cac y duoi day vao bao cao.

## 1. Muc tieu de tai

He thong de xuat mot quy trinh ho tro tao video ban tu dong tu script van ban. Dau vao la file script, dau ra la narration audio, anh minh hoa lien quan, va timeline co the dua vao DaVinci Resolve de phuc vu dung video nhanh hon.

## 2. Kien truc he thong

He thong gom cac khoi:

1. Tien xu ly van ban
2. Phan tich noi dung script
3. Tao narration bang TTS
4. Truy xuat anh minh hoa
5. Xay dung timeline
6. Tich hop DaVinci Resolve

## 3. Quy trinh xu ly

1. Nguoi dung cung cap script text.
2. He thong tach script thanh cac segment ngan.
3. Moi segment duoc phan tich de tim keyword, scene, tone.
4. He thong tao audio narration cho tung segment.
5. He thong tim anh minh hoa phu hop cho tung segment.
6. Timeline duoc tao tu audio va image theo thu tu script.

## 4. Ket qua mong doi

- Giam thoi gian chuan bi media cho video
- Ho tro narrator voice dong bo voi noi dung
- Tu dong hoa mot phan quy trinh dung video
- Tao duoc prototype kha thi de mo rong thanh plugin hoan chinh
